Designed to meet the needs of both a real
and virtual classroom.

Student Can

  • Submit class assignments
  • Discuss course material on a closed or open forum
  • Create petitions and place requests
  • Quickly chat with teachers on exam clearance and other issues

Teachers Can

  • Host a virtual classroom
  • Post updates on class assignments and deadlines
  • Accept coursework
  • Carry out discussions on a closed or open forum
  • Accept or Reject petitions/requests
